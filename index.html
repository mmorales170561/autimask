Welcome to Auti-Mask

This website is dedicated to providing resources, guides, and strategies for individuals with autism spectrum disorder (ASD) and their families. Our goal is to offer information that can help with managing the challenges of ASD and creating positive environments.

Guides for Managing Autism

Behavioral Strategies

Learn effective behavioral techniques for managing challenging behaviors associated with autism. These include positive reinforcement, structured routines, and behavior modification methods.

  • Autism Speaks - Behavioral Therapy
  • National Institute of Health - Autism Behavioral Strategies

Communication Skills

Effective communication is crucial for people with autism. This guide offers tips and resources on how to enhance verbal and nonverbal communication.

  • Autism Speaks - Communication Resources
  • The Communication Trust

Sensory Integration

Individuals with autism may have sensory sensitivities. Learn how to manage sensory challenges, including techniques for creating sensory-friendly environments.

  • National Autistic Society - Sensory sensory-differences
  • Sensory Processing Autism speaks

Light Integration

Individuals with autism may have light sensitivities. Learn how to manage light challenges, including techniques for creating low-light environments.

  • Golden Care Therapy-Light Sensitivity in Autism
  • Theraspecks-Light Sensitivity and Autism Spectrum Disorder
